## QuickAnswer
If you are new to Midjourney, start with short prompts, one style reference, and a clear subject. This gives better results than long, overloaded prompts.

## What Midjourney actually does
Midjourney is a text-to-image tool that is strongest at cinematic compositions, stylized portraits, and mood-heavy visuals. It is used by freelancers for ad creatives, YouTube thumbnails, product mockups, and storyboarding.

For South Asian users, Midjourney is especially useful when you need campaign visuals quickly for local brands, wedding/event creatives, or festival posts in multiple styles.

## Step 1: Account and access
1. Go to [Midjourney](https://www.midjourney.com?ref=toolverse) and sign in.
2. Pick the basic paid plan to start.
3. Open the create interface and test with simple prompts first.

## Step 2: Write your first good prompt
Use this structure:
`[subject], [style], [lighting], [camera angle], [quality cue]`

Examples:
- `a Nepali tea shop street scene, cinematic, rainy evening, wide angle, ultra detailed`
- `bridal jewelry product photo, studio lighting, soft shadows, white backdrop, premium look`
- `modern fintech app illustration, flat vector style, blue palette, clean composition`

## Step 3: Improve output quickly
- Keep prompts short in early iterations.
- Change one variable at a time.
- Save your best prompt versions in a notes file.
- Build a mini style library for your client niches.

## Pricing explained simply
Midjourney starts at an entry plan and scales by generation volume and speed. If you are freelancing, your first upgrade should be based on project frequency, not hype.

## Tips for South Asian creators
- Use location cues: Kathmandu, Delhi, Dhaka, Colombo.
- Add festival context: Dashain, Tihar, Diwali, Eid campaign posters.
- Blend local textiles, architecture, and color language for more authentic output.

## FAQs
### Is Midjourney beginner-friendly?
Yes, if you start with short prompts and iterate in small steps.

### Can I use Midjourney images for client work?
Usually yes on paid plans, but verify license terms before delivery.

### Does Midjourney have a free plan?
No, treat it as a paid creative tool from day one.

### What is the biggest beginner mistake?
Writing very long prompts with conflicting styles.

### Should I choose Midjourney or DALL-E first?
Pick Midjourney for style-heavy art and DALL-E for broader utility.
