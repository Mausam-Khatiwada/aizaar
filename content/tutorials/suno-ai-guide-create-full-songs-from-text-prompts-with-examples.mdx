## QuickAnswer
Good Suno output comes from clear genre + mood + structure instructions. Vague prompts produce generic songs.

## Prompt formula
`Genre + Mood + Tempo + Instruments + Vocal style + Song structure`

Example:
`Modern Nepali pop ballad, emotional but hopeful, 92 BPM, acoustic guitar and soft synth, male vocal, verse-chorus-verse-chorus-bridge-chorus`

## Build better songs in 5 steps
1. Start with 1 clear style direction.
2. Add reference emotions.
3. Define verse and chorus contrast.
4. Generate two versions.
5. Extend the better version.

## Lyrics prompting tips
- Keep hooks short and memorable.
- Mention syllable simplicity for singability.
- Avoid overly complex metaphors in first draft.

## FAQs
### Can Suno generate full songs?
Yes, including vocals and arrangement.

### Should I write full lyrics manually?
Start with AI draft, then rewrite key lines for originality.

### Can I extend a partial song?
Yes, Suno supports continuation workflows.

### Is it good for ads?
Yes, especially for quick concept drafts.

### Suno vs Udio for beginners?
Try both; Suno is often faster for first complete outputs.
