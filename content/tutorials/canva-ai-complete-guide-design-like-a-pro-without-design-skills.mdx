## QuickAnswer
Canva AI works best when you start from a proven template and use AI for variations, not full replacement. This keeps quality high and brand consistency intact.

## Start with the right project
1. Open [Canva AI](https://www.canva.com?ref=toolverse).
2. Pick a format first: post, reel cover, thumbnail, presentation.
3. Add your brand colors and fonts before generating anything.

## Features that matter most
### Magic Design
Paste a short brief and let Canva suggest layouts.

### Text to image
Use for background scenes and mood visuals.

### Background remover
Fast product mockups and portrait edits.

### AI presentation drafts
Useful when clients need urgent deck outlines.

## Freelancer workflow
- Build one master template per niche.
- Generate 3 AI variants for each deliverable.
- Keep one conservative option and two creative options.

## Practical prompt samples
- `Minimal Instagram carousel for personal finance tips, clean typography, blue and white`
- `Luxury salon promo poster with soft gradients and elegant headline spacing`

## FAQs
### Is Canva AI enough for client work?
Yes for most social and marketing deliverables.

### Do I still need Photoshop?
For heavy compositing and precision retouching, yes.

### Can I create presentations in Canva AI?
Yes, and it is one of the fastest use cases.

### Is there a free plan?
Yes, but some AI features are limited.

### How do I keep designs non-generic?
Use your own brand kit and custom copy voice.
