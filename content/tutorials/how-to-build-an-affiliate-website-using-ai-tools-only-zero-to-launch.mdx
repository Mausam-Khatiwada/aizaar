## QuickAnswer
You can launch an affiliate site quickly with AI tools, but the moat comes from editorial quality, comparison depth, and honest recommendations, not volume spam.

## Stack used in this workflow
- Research: [Perplexity AI](/tools/perplexity-ai)
- Writing: [Claude](/tools/claude), [ChatGPT](/tools/chatgpt)
- SEO briefs: [Ahrefs](/tools/ahrefs), [Surfer SEO](/tools/surfer-seo)
- Design: [Canva AI](/tools/canva-ai)
- Build: [Cursor](/tools/cursor)

## Phase 1: Niche and content model
Pick one high-intent niche with obvious purchase decisions. Build a content map around:
- best tools
- tool comparisons
- alternatives
- tutorials

## Phase 2: Production pipeline
1. Generate briefs and SERP outlines.
2. Draft content with AI.
3. Manually add opinions, examples, and warnings.
4. Publish with schema and internal linking.

## Phase 3: Monetization
- Affiliate links in review sections
- Comparison tables with strong intent CTAs
- Newsletter for repeat traffic

## Realistic timeline and costs
- Week 1: setup + first 10 pages
- Week 2: 10-20 supporting pages
- Month 2+: optimize winners and update weekly

## FAQs
### Can AI content rank without edits?
Rarely. Human editorial quality is the differentiator.

### How many pages before traffic starts?
Usually 20-40 quality pages with useful intent coverage.

### What is the biggest mistake?
Publishing generic pages with no unique judgment.

### Should I target global or local traffic first?
Start with one geography and one audience.

### Is this model still worth it in 2026?
Yes, if you prioritize trust and depth over quantity.
