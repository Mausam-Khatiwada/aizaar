## QuickAnswer
Paste clear bullet points, pick a theme close to your brand, and edit only the highest-impact slides. That is the fastest path to a clean presentation in Gamma.

## Step-by-step workflow
1. Open [Gamma](https://gamma.app?ref=toolverse).
2. Paste your raw text or upload notes.
3. Pick a visual style and generate the deck.
4. Edit cover slide, proof slide headlines, and CTA slide.
5. Export as PDF or share live link.

## What Gamma does well
- Fast first-draft generation
- Good typography defaults
- Easy collaborator sharing

## Freelancer tips
- Keep one reusable pitch structure.
- Generate first draft in English, localize after.
- Use one chart per slide to avoid clutter.

## Common mistakes
- Pasting unstructured text blocks.
- Using too many style variations in one deck.
- Skipping manual review before client share.

## FAQs
### Is Gamma better than PowerPoint for speed?
Yes for first draft creation and rapid client iterations.

### Can I export to PDF?
Yes, export and also keep a shared live link.

### Is it useful for freelancers?
Very, especially for proposal decks and audits.

### Does Gamma support team edits?
Yes, collaboration is built in.

### Should I still design manually?
Do manual polish on key slides for better conversion.
