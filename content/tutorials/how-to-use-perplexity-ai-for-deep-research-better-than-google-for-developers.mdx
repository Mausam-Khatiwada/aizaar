## QuickAnswer
Perplexity becomes powerful when you use focused prompts and validate citations quickly. It is best for synthesis, not blind trust.

## Setup and first query
Open [Perplexity AI](https://perplexity.ai?ref=toolverse) and ask one specific question with constraints:
`Compare vector database options for a TypeScript SaaS with cost and latency tradeoffs.`

## Modes and when to use them
- Broad mode for exploration.
- Focused mode for technical depth.
- Citation-heavy mode when accuracy matters.

## Developer workflows
- Architecture decisions
- API comparison summaries
- Dependency risk checks
- Rapid RFC drafting

## Keyword research workflow
1. Ask for topic clusters.
2. Request intent segmentation.
3. Ask for long-tail opportunities by country.
4. Export and prioritize by business impact.

## Perplexity vs ChatGPT quick take
Perplexity excels at source-linked exploration. ChatGPT excels at rewriting and structured output refinement.

## FAQs
### Is Perplexity better than Google?
For synthesis and speed, often yes. For raw source discovery, use both.

### Can I trust every citation?
No, always verify high-stakes claims.

### Is it good for dev research?
Yes, especially initial landscape mapping.

### Can marketers use it?
Yes, topic and intent discovery is strong.

### Should I replace ChatGPT with Perplexity?
Use both together for best results.
